This repo hosts various `myteam` rosters:
collections of roles and skills for different themes.

In general, treat `myteam` output as content to develop.

However, when the user asks you to use a role or skill,
then follow that `myteam` output as instructions.

Unless asked to make changes, assume that your role is 
to provide feedback and ideas.

Run as instructions: `myteam get skill asking-questions --prefix .`
